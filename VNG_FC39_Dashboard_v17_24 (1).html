Key Takeaways — FC3+9

For SMT | April 2026

↩ Recap from FC2+10
  • Group Adj EBIT PB 1,266bn (+9% AP, +19% YoY) at FC2+10 — FC3+9 stable at 1,268bn. Broad-based delivery confirmed.
  • Adj EBITDA inflated by ROUA +67bn (group-wide, ongoing from Feb 2026 — DC2 deconsolidation). EBITDA watch only; EBIT PB unaffected.
  • GreenNode H2 pipeline escalated to ALERT: FC2+10 asked BU to confirm replacement contract (121bn Sep-Dec). No confirmation at FC3+9 — alert maintained.
  • Instantia MTM risk: −86bn unrealized loss as of Q1. Business underperforming vs AP. Direction not yet decided.
  • Corp pricing gap ~11bn/year (DC-STT deconsolidation). Pricing decision still pending SMT resolution.
📊 This Month (FC3+9 vs AP)
  • YTD Adj EBIT Pre Bonus 566bn, +285% vs AP — broad-based outperformance: VNGGames (+127%), ZA (+93%), ZaloPay (+41%).
  • FY Group: 1,268bn (+9% AP / +19% YoY) — forecast stable vs FC2+10. Nearly all BUs at or above AP.
  • ROUA +67bn inflates EBITDA only. Underlying EBITDA beat vs AP = +71bn. Zero impact on Adj EBIT Pre Bonus.
  • VNGGames drives the outperformance: 1,702bn FY (+7% AP, +16% YoY). Costs overrun plan: royalty +1ppt, marketing +38bn, other costs +20bn.
  • NPAT turns positive: 22bn (A25: −118bn, AP: −315bn) — includes one-off disposal gain +219bn from VNG DC decon. Underlying excl. disposal ≈ +112bn.
💡 So What
  • FY profit +8-9% vs AP looks comfortable — but H2 has very little room for downside.
  • VNGGames H2: ~1,070bn GB from unproven new titles (zero Q1 actuals, 8 titles). Evergreen base (3,393bn) holds floor but doesn't grow. Any miss flows straight to bottom line.
  • GreenNode: 121bn Sep-Dec replacement has no signed customer. If replacement fails: revenue gap ~140bn, EBIT PB trends toward −160bn. No upside scenario.
  • ZA revenue mix shifting: High-margin OA shrinking permanently (−57bn); replaced by royalty-bearing VAS. Margin dilution structural, not cyclical.
  • H2 assumes everything goes right — no single-miss buffer. Current forecast has zero contingency margin.
⚡ Call to Action
🔔 ALERT — GreenNode
Replacement pipeline (121bn, Sep-Dec) still unidentified. Potential −160bn swing to Group EBIT PB if unresolved by Q3. BU to provide confirmed contract or downside contingency plan by end of Q2.
📋 ACTION — VNGGames H2
~1,070bn forecast GB across 8 new titles, all zero Q1 actuals. BU to provide: (i) contingency plan with realistic downside scenario, (ii) buffer % per title by launch readiness, (iii) cost discipline commitment — royalty, marketing, channel costs must be controlled against revenue risk.
⚠ OPEN ITEM — Instantia / A4B
Business underperforms vs AP. A4B stopped HR project — not incorporated in FC3+9. SMT to confirm business direction and quantify total P&L impact for FC4+8.
